Son Wukong

Overview

Son Wukong is a character from Chinese Mythology known as the "Monkey King". He is known for his unruly behavior as well as immense powers and vast array of abilities. This individual, born from a magical rock at the top of a mountain was a powerful magical monkey demon who was even able to shake heaven itself, being one of the primary menaces towards the gods.

Appearence

Wukong has the appearence of a normal macaque however, he does have the ability to transform into 72 different things and thus his appearence is maleable. After becoming a warrior and going through his trials and tribulations, Wukong is often depicted as wearing golden armor with iconic pheonix feathers running from his helment as well as holding his golden staff "Ru Yi".

Pop Culture

Wukong has made a variety of appearences in pop culture, while not treated as a true mythological figure by Buddist and Taoists, he has made a huge number of appearences in TV, movies and plays. Perhaps some of the most notable appearences in recent times can include Journey to the West, a famous Chinese film, DragonBall, a famous anime as well as having a very much adapted version in God of High School, another anime. Beyond movie and TV appearences, Wu kong is also commonly found as a character in video games such as League, Dota and Marvel vs. Capcom 2.
