Relationships

Tang Sanzang

Tan Sanzang was the master and teacher of Wukong. Being one of the only inviduals that Wukong actually respects, he is one of the only individuals that is able to control the untempered rage that Wukong had as well as teach him to be a better individual. Tang himself rescued Wukong from the mountain that Buddha pinned him with. After joining him as a discipline, Wukong learned from Tang's teachings, even while controlled by a circlet, however after their Journey to India, Wukong was able to achieve enlightenment.

Jade Emperor

Wukong had a poor relationship with the Jade emperor. Given that the emperor did not respect the power of the Monkey king, he gave the monkey king the lowliest job in heaven and even after the monkey king demonstrated his power, did not treat him as he treated other gods and thus, the monkey king wrecked havok in heaven. It was only through the power of Buddha that he was able to get the monkey king under control. Though this releationship is depicted differently in different forms media the jade emperor typically is shown to have some degree of fear regarding the monkey king.

Buddha

The only being to truely defeat the monkey King, Buddha is more of a omnipotent in these stories. Given his immense power, Buddha sees the monkey king as a mostly interesting little child and does not hesitate to punish him as such.
